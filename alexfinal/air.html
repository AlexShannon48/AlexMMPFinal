Air Pollution

About

Air pollution is when the atmosphere and air is dirtied and polluted by gasses and particles. The main causes of air pollution are factories, car exhaust, and certain burning of materials. When poisonous gasses especiialy from the burning of fossil fuels, are released into the atmosphere, they chip away at the ozone layer (The layer of the atmosphere protecting us from dangerous gasses and radiation) and make it deteriorate. It also leads to climate change which causes mass increases in temperature and erratic, extreme weather. Gasses that cause this include but are not limited to Carbon Monoxide, Radon, and Methane.
